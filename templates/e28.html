BMW E28 M5

The BMW E28 M5, produced from 1984 to 1988, is part of the BMW M series and is known for its performance and sporty character.

Key features of the E28 M5 include:

  • Powered by a 3.5-liter inline-six engine
  • Produced 282 horsepower and 251 lb-ft of torque
  • 5-speed manual transmission
  • Rear-wheel drive
  • 0 to 60 mph in approximately 6.7 seconds

The E28 M5 is recognized for its understated, classic design and remains a sought-after classic car among enthusiasts.
