BMW E34 M5

The BMW E34 M5, produced from 1988 to 1995, is part of the BMW M series and is renowned for its combination of luxury and performance.

Key features of the E34 M5 include:

  • Powered by a 3.6-liter inline-six engine (later models had a 3.8-liter engine)
  • Produced between 311 to 340 horsepower depending on the engine version
  • Available with a 5-speed manual or 6-speed manual transmission
  • Rear-wheel drive
  • 0 to 60 mph in approximately 5.9 seconds (for the 3.8-liter version)

The E34 M5 is known for its refined interior, comfortable ride, and impressive handling, making it a classic among BMW enthusiasts.
