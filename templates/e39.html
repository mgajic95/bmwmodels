BMW E39 M5

The BMW E39 M5, produced from 1998 to 2003, is a part of the BMW M series and is celebrated for its powerful performance and refined luxury.

Key features of the E39 M5 include:

  • Powered by a 4.9-liter V8 engine
  • Produced 394 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque
  • Available with a 6-speed manual transmission
  • Rear-wheel drive
  • 0 to 60 mph in approximately 4.8 seconds

The E39 M5 is known for its elegant design, spacious interior, and impressive handling, making it a favorite among BMW enthusiasts.
